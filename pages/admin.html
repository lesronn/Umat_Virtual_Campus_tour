The Main Administration

The UMaT administration block is the largest and finest block on UMaT campus. It is the centre of administration and houses the esteemed offices of the vice chancellor, pro vice chancellor, deans, registrar and many other student and staff service units. This is a huge complex with about 5 floor compartments with each compartment housing about 10-15 administrative offices. This complex is geographically to the north of the UMaT campus and adjacent the main university entrance
