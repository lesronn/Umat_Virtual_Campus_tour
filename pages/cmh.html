Chamber of Mines Hall

Known as the home of brave and brilliant engineers, the chamber of mines hall is the oldest residential facility on UMaT campus. It comprises of two blocks, Novotel- constructed in 1986 and accommodates 200 students, and Dubai-constructed in 2003 and equally houses 200 students. Each of these blocks has 4 floors. The chamber of mines hall also houses a number of facilities such as: the Junior Common Room(JCR), the SRC gym and the hall assistant lodge and offices, as well as, two printing press. The hall is located to the west of UMaT campus. A 3D model of the chamber of mines hall will be simulated in this project.
